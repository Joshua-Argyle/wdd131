Film Inspect Site Plan

Joshua Argyle

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

To create a site where a large selection of movies can be rated and reviewed to give movie watchers a place to check to see if a movie is worth watching.

Audience

People who watch movies

Dynamic elements

The site would have a form, in which certain aspects of a movie would be rated such as how much action, romance, or comedic elements a movie has from a rating 1 to 10. It would also have the option to search for a specific movie to see the ratings people gave it from the forms people filled out.
